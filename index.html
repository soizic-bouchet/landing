But what is crochet?

What do you need to crochet?

To crochet, you'll need two main things: a hook and some wool. The size of your hook will depend on the thickness of your wool.
To find out what size crochet hook to use, all you need to do is look at the packaging of your wool, where the size required will be indicated.

Where to buy crochet materials?

There are many sites where you can buy your materials. The best-known sellers of wool and crochet are Hobbi, HappyWool, The Wool Factory, for example, but you can also find what you need on Amazon or, to be ecological, go to your local haberdashery.
